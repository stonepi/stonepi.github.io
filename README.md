# Shi Pi's Github Page

My name is **Shi Pi (皮石, 피석, Bì Thạch)**. **Pi**, my family name, is a rare surname in China meaning "skin," "peel," or "decorticate." It is predominantly found in the provinces of Jiangxi, Hunan, and Hubei. Legend traces its origin to Fan Zhong Pi (樊仲皮), a minister (卿士) during the Western Zhou Dynasty (1046 – 771 BC). His lineage includes the renowned Tang Dynasty poet Pi Ri-Xiu (皮日休, 834 – 883), author of the ten-volume collection *Pizi Wensou* and co-author of *Songling Ji*, a collection of responsory poems with his contemporary Lu Gui-Meng (陸龜蒙). The surname also has a presence in Korea. Notably, So-Young Pi, a professor of physics at Boston University, is a pioneer of inflationry cosmology. Her father Cheon-deuk Pi is a celebrated Korean writer.
